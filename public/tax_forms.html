Choose the forms you need to file your taxes. Start by selecting the primary form and then add any schedules or other forms you require.

Form 1040

U.S. Individual Income Tax Return. This is the primary form for most taxpayers.

Schedule 1 (Form 1040)

Schedule A (Form 1040)

Schedule B (Form 1040)

Wage and Tax Statement. For employees to report their wages and tax withheld.

Miscellaneous Income. For reporting payments made in the course of a trade or business.
