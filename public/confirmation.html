Frequently Asked Questions

When will I receive my refund?

Refund timelines vary depending on how you filed and the complexity of your return. Generally, electronic filings are processed faster than paper filings. You can track your refund status on the IRS website.

How do I download a copy of my tax return?

A PDF copy of your filed tax return is available in the confirmation email. You can also download it from your account dashboard.

What if I need to make changes to my return?

If you need to make changes, you can file an amended tax return (Form 1040-X). Detailed instructions are available in our help section.
